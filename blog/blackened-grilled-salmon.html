Blackened Grilled Salmon Feast

Crispy-edged blackened salmon with a creamy dill yogurt sauce, rice pilaf, and grilled asparagus. This is one of those “looks impressive, cooks fast” meals.
Grill Weeknight High-protein
Home Food Projects
Blackened salmon: high heat, short cook, rest briefly, then sauce.

Method

  1. Prep. Pat salmon dry. Lightly oil, then season generously with blackening spice (or paprika + garlic + cayenne + salt + pepper).
  2. Preheat grill. Medium-high. Clean grates well and oil them (fish sticks when you get lazy here).
  3. Grill. Skin-side down first. Close lid. Cook until it releases easily, then flip briefly to finish (or don’t flip if it’s thin).
  4. Rest. 2–3 minutes so juices settle.
  5. Sauce + sides. Spoon on dill sauce. Serve with rice pilaf and grilled asparagus.

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets (skin-on recommended)
  • Oil (avocado or olive)
  • Blackening seasoning (or paprika + garlic + cayenne + salt + pepper)
  • Fresh dill
  • Plain yogurt (or mayo/yogurt blend)
  • Lemon (juice + zest)
  • Garlic (microplaned)
  • Rice pilaf + asparagus (to serve)

Dill yogurt sauce

Mix yogurt + lemon + garlic + chopped dill + salt/pepper. Thin with a splash of water or olive oil if needed.
If you want it richer, do half yogurt / half mayo.
