How this will work

I’m aiming for two tiers:

  • Public highlights — curated albums that are safe to share.
  • Private vault — originals hosted from my home machine behind Cloudflare Zero Trust access controls.
The private vault is intentionally gated. No open ports, no “public directory listing,” and access will be restricted by identity (or at least a strong auth layer).
